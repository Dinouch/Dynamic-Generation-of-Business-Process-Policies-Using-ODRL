# Dynamic-Generation-of-Business-Process-Policies-Using-ODRL

This repository is part of my Final Engineering Project in Information Systems.
It addresses a real-world challenge frequently encountered in organizations: ensuring coordination and governance across distributed fragments of business processes.

The project proposes a solution that dynamically generates ODRL (Open Digital Rights Language) policies directly from natural language descriptions, using Large Language Models (LLMs).
The goal is to bridge the gap between business-level constraints expressed by humans and machine-readable governance rules, enabling more flexible, transparent, and automated process execution.

The system takes BPMN fragments or textual business rules as input, applies fragmentation strategies, and produces corresponding ODRL policies that can be used for policy-based orchestration of distributed business processes.
